# ⬡ ChemoFilter: Business Model Canvas & Commercialization

**Transitioning from a Research Snapshot to a Pharmaceutical SaaS Suite**  
*Strategic Plan for Scaling ChemoFilter post-MDP 2026*

---

## 1. Executive Summary: The "Value Proposition"

Current drug discovery software is either:
1.  **Expensive (Proprietary):** Subscriptions cost $100k+/year (e.g., Schrodinger).
2.  **Fragmented (Open-Source):** Requires multiple Python environments and complex installation.

**ChemoFilter** fills the "Missing Middle": A professional, interactive, and **Offline-First** ADMET profiler that empowers any medicinal chemist to triage their own leads without needing a dedicated computational chemistry team.

## 3. The "Institutional Enterprise" Tier (Tier 11)

Post-MDP, the "Institutional" version of ChemoFilter will include:

1.  **Shared Dashboards:** Real-time collaboration between lab members.
2.  **Private PDB Vaults:** Uploading proprietary receptor structures for local docking.
3.  **Local LLMs:** Running Llama-3 or Mistral locally to keep AI rationales 100% air-gapped.
