## 1. Executive Overview

Current drug discovery software is either **Expensive** or **Fragmented**. **ChemoFilter** fills the "Missing Middle": A professional, interactive, and **Offline-First** ADMET profiler that empowers any medicinal chemist to triage their own leads without needing a dedicated computational chemistry team.

It costs **$2.6 Billion** and **10+ years** to bring a single drug to market. 90% of candidates fail in Phase II/III clinical trials due to unforeseen toxicological liabilities, off-target promiscuity, or poor pharmacokinetic profiles.

**1. 9-Tier Processing Engine:** Progressive triage from 2D structures to 3D quantum descriptors.
