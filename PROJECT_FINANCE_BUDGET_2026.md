## 3. Recurring Operational Costs (SaaS Model)

The "Offline-First" philosophy keeps operational costs low:

*   **API Usage (LLMs):** ~$0.02 per molecule interpreted via **Anthropic Claude 3**.
*   **Infrastructure:** ~$200/month for the **FastParquet Hub** and documentation wiki.
*   **Maintenance:** One Senior Backend Engineer (Part-time) for RDKit versioning.

## 5. ROI for Institutional Users (The "Lead Advantage")

ChemoFilter pays for itself by reducing "Wasted Synthesis Time."

*   **Average Cost of Synthesis:** ~$2,500 per compound.
*   **ChemoFilter Cost:** Less than $0.50 (Compute + API).
*   **ROI Logic:** By identifying a "Toxic" or "Insoluble" compound *before* synthesis, a lab saves **$2,499.50** per molecule triaged.

## 6. Conclusion: A High-Impact, Low-Burn Rate Platform

Unlike traditional cheminformatics suites requiring millions in upfront licensing, ChemoFilter provides a high-impact research capability with a low-burn rate. It is a viable investment for biotech incubators and university research centers seeking to modernize their scientific infrastructure for the **MDP 2026 era.**
