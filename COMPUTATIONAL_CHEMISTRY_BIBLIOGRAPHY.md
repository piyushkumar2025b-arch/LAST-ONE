## 2. ADMET & Molecular Descriptors (Tiers 1–3)

1.  **Hansch, C., & Leo, A. (1995).** 
    *Exploring QSAR: Fundamentals and Applications in Chemistry and Biology.* American Chemical Society.
    > Logic: Foundation for **LogP** and the additive-fragment constant model ($f$-constant).
2.  **Delaney, J. S. (2004).**
    *ESOL: Estimating Aqueous Solubility Directly from Molecular Structure.* Journal of Chemical Information and Computer Sciences.
    > Logic: Linear regression model for **Aqueous Solubility (logS)** in Tier 3 (Hyper-Zenith).
3.  **Bickerton, G. R., et al. (2012).**
    *Quantifying the Chemical Beauty of Drugs.* Nature Chemistry.
    > Logic: Desirability functions for the **QED (Quantitative Estimate of Drug-likeness)** score.
