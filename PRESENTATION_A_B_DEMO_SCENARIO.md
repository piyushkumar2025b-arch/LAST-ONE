# ⬡ ChemoFilter: A/B Presentation Demo Scenario

**Comparing "Chemical Junk" vs. "High-Quality Leads" Live**  
*The Script for the VIT MDP 2026 Competitive Showcase*

---

## 1. Executive Overview

The most effective way to "WOW" a judge during a 5-minute demo is to show a direct comparison. This document outlines the **A/B Demo Scenario**: Comparing a "Bad Lead" (High Toxicity/Poor Solubility) with a "Good Lead" (High Lead Score).

---

## 2. Preparation (The "Anchor" Molecules)

*   **Molecule A (The Failure):** `C1=CC=C2C(=C1)C=CC3=CC=CC=C32` (Pyrene—Highly aromatic, insoluble, potentially toxic).
*   **Molecule B (The Success):** `CC(=O)OC1=CC=CC=C1C(=O)O` (Aspirin—Proven, soluble, passed Ro5).

---

## 3. Demo Step 1: Input & Instant Triage (Tier 1)

1.  **Action:** Paste **Molecule A** into the SMILES field.
2.  **Observation:** Tier 1 (**Vanguard Core**) flags it with **"LogP > 5.0 Liability"** and **"High Aromatic Proportion."**
3.  **Action:** Paste **Molecule B** into the SMILES field.
4.  **Observation:** Tier 1 flags it as **"Lipinski Pass (0 Violations)."**

---

## 4. Demo Step 2: Scientific Depth (Tiers 3–7)

1.  **Action:** Navigate to the **"Physicochemical Constraint Laboratory."**
2.  **Molecule A (Pyrene):** Show the **ESOL (logS)** warning: 🔴 **"Insoluble (-6.5)."**
3.  **Molecule B (Aspirin):** Show the **ESOL (logS)** success: 🟢 **"Soluble (-1.5)."**
4.  **Action:** Navigate to the **"Hazard Overlay Map"** (Tier 7).
5.  **Molecule A (Pyrene):** Show the **hERG** liability.
6.  **Molecule B (Aspirin):** Show the **hERG** safety.

---

## 5. Demo Step 3: AI Rationale & The "Aha!" Moment

1.  **Action:** Navigate to the **"Mechanistic Logic (AI)"** tab.
2.  **Molecule A:** The AI should say: *"Lead rejected due to extreme lipophilicity and poor aqueous solubility. High hazard potential for cardiotoxicity."*
3.  **Molecule B:** The AI should say: *"Excellent candidate. Passes all core ADMET filters with high synthetic accessibility. A proven analgesic scaffold."*

---

## 6. Closing the Loop: The "Final Research Report"

1.  **Action:** Scroll to the bottom of the **"Showcase Summary"** tab.
2.  **Result:** Click the **"Generate Final PDF"** button.
3.  **The "Wow" Factor:** Present the judge with a 1-page summary showing exactly why **Molecule B** is the winner and **Molecule A** is "Chemical Junk," saved directly as a .PDF with the **Crystalline Obsidian** branding.
