# ⬡ ChemoFilter: Experimental Validation Protocol

**Connecting "In Silico" Predictions to "In Vitro" Realities**  
*The Wet-Lab Sequence for Verifying ChemoFilter Leads*

---

## 1. Executive Overview

Computational triage (ChemoFilter) is the first filter. The final validation of any "High Lead Score" compound requires empirical testing in a laboratory. This document outlines the standard experimental sequence to verify the ADMET predictions generated by the **9-Tier Engine**.

---

## 2. Phase 1: Physicochemical Verification

Before biological testing, the physical constants must be verified:

1.  **Aqueous Solubility (logS):** Shake-flask method followed by HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) to verify the **Tier 3 (ESOL)** prediction.
2.  **LogP (Partition Coefficient):** Octanol-Water partition test to verify the **Tier 1 (Vanguard)** lipophilicity calculation.
3.  **pKa (Ionization):** Potentiometric titration to verify the **Tier 7 (Celestial)** ionization state prediction.

---

## 3. Phase 2: Metabolic Stability (Microsomal Assay)

To verify the **Tier 7 (Specific CYP Inhibition)** predictions:

*   **Logic:** Incubate the lead compound with human or rat liver microsomes ($HLM/RLM$) in the presence of NADPH.
*   **Result:** Use LC-MS/MS to identify the half-life ($T_{1/2}$) and the clearance rate ($CL_{int}$), comparing it directly to the **Celestial Engine** output.

---

## 4. Phase 3: Toxicity & Safety (Cell-Based Assays)

1.  **hERG Inhibition:** Manual patch-clamp or fluorescence-based assays on HEK293 cells expressing the hERG channel. (Validates **Tier 7 hERG liability**).
2.  **Cytotoxicity (HepG2):** MTT or LDH assays to determine the $IC_{50}$ of the drug on human liver cells. (Validates **Tier 9 Toxicological Hazard**).

---

## 5. Phase 4: Binding Affinity ($K_d$ / $IC_{50}$)

To verify the **Tier 9 (Docking Energy)** or **Target Class** predictions:

*   **Assay:** Surface Plasmon Resonance (SPR) or isothermal titration calorimetry (ITC).
*   **Significance:** Measures the exact binding strength ($K_d$) and confirms if the drug is a "Surgical Strike" or a "Non-Specific Binder."

---

## 6. Closing the Loop: Updating the Engine

If the experimental result differs from the ChemoFilter prediction:
1.  Navigate to `data_engine.py`.
2.  Add the experimental value to the **"Verified Lead Repository."**
3.  Re-train the Tier 7/9 regression model using the new "Active Data," ensuring the engine becomes more accurate with every lab test.
