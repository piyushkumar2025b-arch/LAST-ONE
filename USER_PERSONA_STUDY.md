# ⬡ ChemoFilter: User Persona Study & Stakeholder Analysis

**Designing for the Multidisciplinary Drug Discovery Pipeline**  
*Understanding the Interface Needs of Different User Groups*

---

## 1. Overview: The Audience for ChemoFilter

ChemoFilter was not built for a single user. Its 40+ Analytical Tabs and 9-Tier engine were engineered to address the specific pain points of three distinct scientific profiles.

---

## 2. Persona 1: The Medicinal Chemist (Dr. Aris Thorne)

**Background:** PhD in Organic Chemistry. Spends 80% of their time synthesizing novel scaffolds in the lab.
**Key Objective:** "Will this molecule I've designed actually pass into the cell, or am I wasting a week on a brick?"

*   **Priority Tabs:** 
    *   **🧪 Physicochemical Constraint Laboratory:** To check Lipinski compliance.
    *   **🔀 Scaffold Morphing:** To see alternative side-chains that maintain potency but reduce molecular weight.
*   **How ChemoFilter Helps:** Provides the **SA_Score** (Synthetic Accessibility). If a molecule's SA is $>5$, Aris knows it’s too complex to synthesize with current reagents.

---

## 3. Persona 2: The Computational Toxicologist (Maya Lin)

**Background:** Data Scientist focusing on safety pharmacology. Responsible for red-flagging compounds before they reach animal trials.
**Key Objective:** "Does this molecule have any structural motifs that might trigger cardiotoxicity or mutagenicity?"

*   **Priority Tabs:**
    *   **⬡ hERG Risk Analyst:** To check for QT-prolongation potential.
    *   **⬡ PAINS Alert Console:** To identify molecules that might generate "false signals" in screenings.
*   **How ChemoFilter Helps:** Mayan utilizes the **Tier 7 (Celestial Engine)** to run deep-tissue kinetic simulations and epigenetic hazard scanning.

---

## 4. Persona 3: The Lab Research Manager (Director Sam Vane)

**Background:** Senior executive at a Biotech startup. Manages the budget and the R&D pipeline.
**Key Objective:** "Which of these 100 potential leads should we prioritize for our next round of funding?"

*   **Priority Tabs:**
    *   **⬡ Screening Summary & Analytical Dashboard:** The high-level view.
    *   **✅ Final Research Conclusion:** The AI-summarized rationale.
*   **How ChemoFilter Helps:** Sam relies on the **Lead Score**. A higher score (e.g., 85+) justifies the allocation of capital and resources to that specific compound.

---

## 5. UI/UX Tailoring: The "Crystalline Obsidian" Philosophy

The different needs of these personas are harmonized through the "Crystalline Obsidian" design system:

1.  **Complexity on Demand:** For Sam (Manager), the high-level cards are front-and-center. For Maya (Toxicologist), the detailed "Hazard Tables" are accessible via a single click.
2.  **Visual Confirmation:** The **3D Conformational Force-Field Explorer** allows Aris (Chemist) to rotate the molecule and see its spatial "bulk," which is critical for understanding receptor fit.
